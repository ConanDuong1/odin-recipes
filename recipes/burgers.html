Burger

Description

Here is one of the best ways to make a classic juicy burger fit for a commercial.

Ingredients

  • Sesame Hamburger Buns
  • Bacon
  • Ground Beef
  • Salt and Pepper
  • American Cheese
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • White Onion

Steps

  1. Toast your buns
  2. Increase the heat and cook your bacon
  3. Make a ball with your ground beef and flatten it, seasoning with salt and pepper
  4. Cook the seasoned side and season the other side
  5. Lay cheese on the patty when it's almost done
  6. Transfer patty to bottom bun
  7. Add toppings
  8. Serve!
