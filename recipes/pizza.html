Pizza

Description

Here is a way to make a classic American style pizza right in your home!

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Tomato Paste
  • Dried Oregano
  • Dried Basil
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Oil
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425F
  2. Grease your pizza pans
  3. Whisk together your sauce ingredients
  4. Combine flour, sugar, yeast, salt, water, and oil and mix until dough forms
  5. Knead until smooth and elastic
  6. Divide dough and form pizza shape
  7. Add toppings
  8. Bake until brown
