Lasagna

Description

Here is a lasagna recipe that I pulled off the internet for The Odin Project!

Lasagna is one of my favorite foods that is worth every bit of the labor.

Ingredients

  • Meat (Grounded)
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • 2 Cans of Tomato Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Sugar
  • Spices (Parsley, Dried Basil, Salt, Italian Seasoning, Pepper)
  • Cheese
  • Pasta Noodles

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna!
  5. Cover with foil
  6. Let lasagna rest
